
http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/register
	说明：注册链接
	参数：userName, password, phone
	例子：http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/register?userName=luyunming&password=1234567890&phone=15708464677
	
http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/isNameExit
	检测用户名是否已存在
	参数：userName
	例子：http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/isNameExit?userName=luyunming
	
http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/isPhoneExit
	说明：检测手机号是否已被绑定
	参数：phone
	例子：http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/isPhoneExit?phone=15708464677
	
http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/login
	说明：依据用户名与密码登录
	参数：userName, password
	例子：http://localhost:8080/RemoteBuilderService/login?userName=luyunming&password=1234567890
